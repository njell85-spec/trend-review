🥇Cefazolin vs Antistaphylococcal Penicillin for MSSA Bacteremia
MSSA 균혈증에서 세파졸린 vs 항포도상구균 페니실린 — SNAP Trial
N Engl J Med, 2026 · PMID 42308484 · 적용성 9.5/10 · RCT
Why It Matters

Clinical Bottom Line
